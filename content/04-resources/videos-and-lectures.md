## A note on use

Short videos are best as openers or companions, not as the substance of a session. A four-minute explainer can surface a shared reference the room then complicates; a lecture course can carry a reading group through a difficult text over weeks. Name the framing a video brings, especially when it is mainstream or liberal, so the room reads it critically rather than absorbing it.
