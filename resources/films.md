## A note on selection

Films can do work that text cannot. They show. They put viewers in proximity to people and places that words describe at a distance. They also have their own ideology, their own framings, their own omissions. Sessions built around films benefit from explicit discussion of what the film does and does not show.

## A note on access

Many of the documentaries the school uses are available on streaming services or for free online (PBS, the Internet Archive, on filmmakers' own sites). Others require purchase or library access. Where a film is hard to access, the school notes this.
