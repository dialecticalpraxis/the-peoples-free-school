# Podcasts

Ongoing podcasts and individual episodes the school draws on. Podcasts are useful for adult learners because they fit into commutes, chores, and other time that otherwise cannot be used for study.

## How to use this index

Entries are organized first by ongoing podcasts (where multiple episodes are useful) and then by individual episodes worth knowing.

## Ongoing podcasts

> To be populated. Each entry should include the show name, host or producer, brief description of focus, and which themes it relates to.

## Individual episodes

> To be populated. Each entry should include podcast name, episode title, date, brief description, and relevant theme(s) and lens(es).

## A note on selection

The school is not in the business of endorsing podcasts wholesale. Many useful podcasts have episodes that miss, and many uneven podcasts have episodes that are excellent. Curate by episode where possible.
